What We're About

The Social Club (TSC) isn’t another boring club — it’s an experience hub. We create spaces where students step out of their shells, try new things, and make real connections. No pressure. No awkward icebreakers. Just good vibes and cool experiences.

Why Join TSC?

Connect with Everyone

Meet people you’d never normally cross paths with and build genuine friendships.

Fresh Experiences

Do fun, unexpected activities that make each meetup memorable — not your usual bingo stuff.

Build Confidence

Grow socially in a natural, laid-back way without feeling forced or judged.

Join a Movement

Be part of something that feels more like a movement than a club — shake up campus life.

What We Do

Experience Nights

From silent discos to DIY escape rooms, we host events that feel like adventures.

Social Experiments

Group missions that shake up campus life — compliment bombs, flash hangouts, unlikely pairings.

Creative Challenges

Hot take debates, story swaps, mini film nights — unleash your creativity with friends.

Flash Hangouts

Random pop-up events announced day-of, just for fun and spontaneity.

How to Join

1. Show up to a TSC event (you’ll hear about it on flyers, socials, and word of mouth).
2. Bring yourself (and maybe a friend).
3. Leave with a story to tell.

Stay tuned for event drops and updates. Follow us on social media or join our Discord for more details.
